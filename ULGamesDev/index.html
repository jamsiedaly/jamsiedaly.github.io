UL Game Jam 24th March

The University of Limerick Games Development Society is hosting a Game Jam on the 24th of March 2018.

The event will run from 10am to 10pm in the CSIS building on the UL campus. All game makers welcome.

We accept games of every kind wheter you choose to use an advanced engine like Unreal or you would like to keep it old school with arts and crafts, there'll be room for you at our game jam. Prizes and sponsors to be announced soon.
